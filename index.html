Berastagi Oranges

Berastagi oranges, also known as Mandarin oranges or tangerines, are a type of citrus fruit native to Indonesia. They are small and round, with a thin, easy-to-peel skin that ranges in color from orange to yellow. Berastagi oranges are known for their sweet, juicy flesh and easy-to-eat segments.
